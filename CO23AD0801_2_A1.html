Fun Facts

Compared to humans, eagles have eyes that are four times sharper.

Hummingbirds can beat their wings 200 times per second.

Among wild cats, tigers are the biggest.

Zebras live in large groups called herds. A 'super herd' may form as they migrate to new feeding grounds.

During the night, hippos forage for food. Grass is their main food source, as they are herbivores.

Know Your Garden Birds!

House sparrow - Small flocks of these noisy birds frequently visit gardens. Usually found in cities, parks, and farms, they eat buds, grains, nuts, and scraps.

Magpie - Noisy magpies are jacks of all trades - scavengers, predators and garden pest gobblers.

Awesome Dogs

1/3 of all households around the world have a dog. These playful, friendly, loyal animals make great companions, but they can also be tough protectors, or intelligent helpers. Dogs were the first animal tamed by humans, over 20,000 years ago! They have evolved from wolves.
